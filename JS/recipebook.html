Pasta Recipe

This is a simple pasta recipe:

  • Boil water in a pot.
  • Add salt and pasta to boiling water.
  • Cook pasta until al dente.
  • Drain pasta and toss with your favorite sauce.
  • Serve hot and enjoy!

Pizza Recipe

This is a simple pizza recipe:

  • Prepare pizza dough.
  • Spread pizza sauce over the dough.
  • Add desired toppings like cheese, veggies, and meats.
  • Bake in preheated oven until crust is golden brown.
  • Slice and serve hot!

Salad Recipe

This is a simple salad recipe:

  • Wash and chop your favorite greens.
  • Add chopped vegetables like tomatoes, cucumbers, and bell peppers.
  • Toss salad with olive oil and vinegar dressing.
  • Season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  • Serve chilled!
